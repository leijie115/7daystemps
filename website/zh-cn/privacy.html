Last updated: 1/31/2026

At 中国气温排行, we prioritize the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by 中国气温排行 and how we use it.

Log Files

We use standard log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, we use "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to www.website.com and other sites on the internet.
